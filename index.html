Visualizing relation between misinformation and public sentiment on Covid-19 vaccine

Visualizing topics and sentiments

The data is visualized in two separate SVGs; one at the top is the topics and then at the bottom is the sentiments. The visualizations change when user changes the month using the slider. User can also highlight specific user's sentiment by selecting from the dropdown.
